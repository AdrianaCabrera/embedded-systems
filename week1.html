EMBEDDED SYSTEMS

Introduction

Recap and creative techniques

During this class we explore the following topics: electricity concepts

* units of measurement of voltage, current, resistance, capacitance, inductance. use of the multimeter.

* how to relate these concepts to electronic components and the use of different conductive and semi-conductive materials. We presented different materials and techniques to develop circuits. finally each student developed his own circuit and tested it.

* We introduced a way to document the projects week by week through the wikifactory platform.

  • Wikifactory


















    • Assignment:

    1. design your circuit in your style

    base on the presented circuits (simple, parallel, rotary switch)you will recreate your circuit by putting your imagination into it, i.e. you will design it in your own way with your own graphics and style, using the techniques presented.

    *conductive ink

    *conductive threads

    *copper tape

    *3D filament in a very simple way, you will pass the soldering hurdles.

    -> Remember that the wires and ink are not solderable, so you will have to do some mechanical bonding to make it work.
