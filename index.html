Designing Embedded Systems

Digital Product Design
HBK Essen Sommer Semester 2021

From simple circuits to design PCBs, in different Formats,in this class we will explorediferent materials and including free alternatives to modify Advanced microcontroller Boards, using both logic schematics and physical board layout editing. Let the students be able to design and produce a circuit on their own.
In this class, we will introduce the concept of embedding systems to understand more about hardware and how to build different embedded systems creatively, where digital product design can have infinite possibilities.
